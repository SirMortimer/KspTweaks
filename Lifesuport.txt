Extended Life Support with Chemical Plants

Mission Profile: 5 Kerbals

Required Processors:
------------------------------
WaterElectrolysis: 1
Sabatier: 1 (dump excess LiquidFuel)
WaterRecycler: 1 (dump excess CO2 and Ammonia)

Resource Tanks:
-----------------------------
Waste Water: empty, as small as possible
CO2: empty, as small as possible
Hydrogen: empty, as small as possible
--------------
8 parts, 1,690kg (1130kg without the LSS unit)

A vessel with just a water recycler can support the same crew for 7 about years when it packs that mass of oxygen.
